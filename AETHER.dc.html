The Story

Crafted from Light.

A fragrance house with one obsession: the feeling of clean light on water, and the optimism of a sky that goes on forever.

I · Light

It begins as light on water — the exact glow of an afternoon you only half remember.

II · Water

Then water. Mineral, cold and clean, like a breath you hold just below the surface.

III · Air

Then air in motion — ozone, frost, the bright lift in the seconds before rain.

IV · Essence

What remains is optimism, distilled — a scent you can almost see straight through.

“AETHER is the air at the very top of the sky. We bottle the way it feels to be there.”
